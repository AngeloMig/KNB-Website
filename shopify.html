FAQ

Shopify questions

Everything worth knowing before we build your store. Still unsure? Ask us directly — it's free.

Book a free consultation
Can I manage the store myself after launch?+

Yes — Shopify is built for it. We set everything up and show you how to add products, run promos and edit pages without touching code.

Do you migrate from another platform?+

We migrate stores from WooCommerce, Wix, Squarespace and others — products, content and (where possible) your URLs, so you keep your SEO.

How long does a Shopify store take?+

Most stores take about 2–4 weeks depending on the number of products and features. We confirm the exact timeline in your written proposal.

Which apps and integrations do you set up?+

Whatever your store needs — email, reviews, subscriptions, shipping and analytics. We keep the stack lean so the store stays fast.
