The challenge

Lumen Apparel had grown its catalog faster than its storefront could keep up. Product pages were cluttered, the navigation buried key collections, and a multi-step checkout was losing shoppers on mobile — where most of the traffic came from.

What we did

We rebuilt the theme around clear product storytelling — bigger imagery, focused product pages, and consistent collection layouts. We restructured the navigation around how customers actually shop, compressed media for speed, and streamlined the checkout to two clean steps.

Throughout, we kept the store easy for the Lumen team to manage, with reusable sections and handoff guidance so they could update content without a developer.

The result

A faster, cleaner storefront that's easier to browse on any device — and noticeably simpler for the team to run day to day. (Replace with the client's own words or agreed, verifiable outcomes — no invented metrics.)
