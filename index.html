FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything worth knowing before we start. Still can't find your answer? Ask us directly — it's free.

Book a free consultation
How long does a project take?+

Most websites and store revamps take about 2–6 weeks depending on scope, number of pages or products, and how quickly content is ready. We confirm the timeline in your proposal.

Which platform is right for me?+

It depends on your goals. Shopify suits most ecommerce brands, WooCommerce/WordPress fits content-heavy or flexible business sites, and Webflow is great for custom marketing sites. We'll recommend the best fit during your free consultation.

How much does a website cost?+

Every project is quoted up front. Pricing depends on scope — the number of pages or products and the features you need — and you'll get a single fixed quote in your written proposal, with no hourly surprises.

What's included in a project?+

Everything is spelled out in the written proposal — scope, deliverables, timeline, and payment terms — so there are no surprises. Common inclusions are design, build, testing, and launch support.

Who owns the website and files?+

You do. On final payment you get full ownership of the website, its content, and all platform accounts — nothing stays locked to us.

Do you write content and provide images?+

We'll guide your content structure and can use high-quality stock imagery to launch. Full copywriting and custom photography can be added if you'd like us to handle it end to end.

Do you offer support after launch?+

Yes. We offer monthly care plans covering updates, backups, speed and security, content edits, and SEO improvements — so your site keeps performing after it goes live.

How do we get started?+

Book a free consultation. We'll learn your goals — and review your current site if you have one — suggest practical improvements, and send a clear proposal if it's a good fit. No pressure either way.
