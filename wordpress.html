FAQ

WordPress questions

Everything worth knowing before we start. Still unsure? Ask us directly — it's free.

Book a free consultation
Can I edit pages and posts myself?+

Absolutely — WordPress is made for it. We build with a clean editor / page builder so you can update content, blog posts and products without a developer.

Do you build WooCommerce stores?+

Yes. WooCommerce turns WordPress into a full online store — products, payments, shipping and tax — with complete control over how it works.

Is WordPress secure and fast?+

It is when it's set up right. We harden security, add backups, optimise speed and keep the plugin stack lean — and our care plans keep it that way.

Do you migrate or redesign an existing WordPress site?+

Both. We redesign tired sites and migrate from other platforms, keeping your content and SEO intact.
